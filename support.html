Stackly Support Ticketing Tool

Stackly provides a fast, automated, AI-powered ticketing solution for IT teams to manage issues, resolve problems, and keep business operations running 24/7.

Smart Support Features

AI Ticket Classification

Stackly identifies the issue type, urgency, and assigns it automatically.

Smart Team Routing

Tickets reach the right engineer instantly based on skill, workload, and SLA.

Self-Healing Automation

Stackly automatically solves common issues with diagnostic scripts.

Support Ticket Workflow

1. User Creates a Ticket

Ticket created through portal, email, or alert system.

2. AI Reads and Understands Issue

NLP analyzes problem, user history, urgency, tone.

3. Ticket Assigned to Engineer

Based on skillset, workload, priority, SLA timer.

4. Automation Runs Diagnostics

Stackly attempts auto-repair: clearing cache, restarting API, rebooting service.

5. Resolution & Closure

Fix applied and user confirms closure.

Stackly Tickets helps teams track issues, resolve problems faster, and deliver smooth IT support.
